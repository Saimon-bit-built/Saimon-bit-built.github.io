Can an Ascendant coach really help me?

If you're Iron through Diamond — absolutely. Ascendant is the top ~2% of the ladder, and the mistakes keeping you stuck are ones I've already made and fixed. Coaching is about diagnosing your gameplay, not the coach's rank flexing.

What do I need for a session?

Discord, and for VOD reviews a recording of your game (free tools like Medal.tv or Valorant's own replay work fine — I'll help you set it up).

What roles and agents do you coach?

Every role — my mains are Omen and Clove (controller), Reyna and Phoenix (duelist), Sage and Killjoy (sentinel). Whatever you main, I've put serious hours on it.

What region and times are you available?

Poland, EU servers, CET. Message me on Discord and we'll find a time — other regions welcome for VOD reviews, since those don't need us to queue together.
